# TruthPulse Store Listing Content

## App Store (iOS)

### App Name
TruthPulse

### Subtitle (30 chars max)
Prediction Market Search

### Promotional Text (170 chars max)
Search 40,000+ open Kalshi prediction markets instantly. Live odds, trend data, and Spotlight integration. No account needed.

### Description (4000 chars max)
TruthPulse gives you instant access to every open prediction market on Kalshi. Type a question and get live odds in seconds.

INSTANT SEARCH
Search across 40,000+ open prediction markets by title, category, or outcome. Results appear as you type with a 4-character minimum and smart ranking that surfaces the most relevant, most liquid markets first.

LIVE ODDS
Every result shows the current YES/NO probability inline. See what the market thinks about elections, economics, sports, weather, and more at a glance.

TREND DATA
Select any market to see price history over 1 day, 7 days, or 30 days. Sparkline charts and delta indicators show you which way odds are moving.

SMART SEARCH
Synonym expansion understands that "fed" means "Federal Reserve", "btc" means "Bitcoin", and dozens more. Field-weighted ranking prioritizes title matches and boosts high-volume markets.

SPOTLIGHT INTEGRATION
TruthPulse indexes all markets into iOS Spotlight. Search for any prediction market right from your home screen without opening the app.

NO ACCOUNT REQUIRED
TruthPulse uses Kalshi's public API. No login, no account, no personal data collected. Tap any result to view the full market on kalshi.com.

PRIVACY FIRST
Zero tracking, zero analytics, zero data collection. Market data is cached on your device for fast search. Nothing leaves your phone.
